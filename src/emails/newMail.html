PROCESS NOTE / SOP DOCUMENTATION ADVISORY
 
PURPOSE
Ensure all functions maintain standardized, updated, and accurate Process Notes/SOPs to improve clarity, control, and audit readiness.

PN/SOP documentation helps:
  • A quick reference guide for new joiner, enabling faster induction and improved productivity.
  • Ensure consistent/common approach to be followed across locations.
  • Cross-department to understand the process & collaborate effectively.
REVIEW & VERSION CONTROL
  • Review every 2 years or earlier if regulatory/process changes occur.
  • Maintain version details such version, date, summary, approver.
APPROVAL WORKFLOW
1. Draft prepared by functional owner.
2. Risk & Compliance review, as required.
3. Final approval by Unit Head/Approver.
4. Maintain audit trail (Teams/Email).
STORAGE & ACCESS
• Store approved SOPs in SharePoint/Repository.
• Maintain list of current & previous versions.
• Share on a need-to-know basis.
RESPONSIBILITIES
Units: Prepare, update SOPs, conduct review.
Other Stakeholders: Review roles/responsibilities in the process.
Risk & Compliance: Review risks/controls/regulatory requirements.
CONCLUSION
A standardized and approved process documentation strengthens governance, reduces risk, and supports operational discipline.
